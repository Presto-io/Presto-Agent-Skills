# v1.15.1 Post-Close Patch

## Purpose

This patch records a post-close formatting issue found after the v1.15 milestone
was archived and after teacher-facing delivery review. The fix stays inside the
shipped v1.15 `teaching-design-package` scope: the package-owned formal
`教学设计方案` renderer must preserve hour labels and align cover metadata labels.

This patch does not reopen the v1.15 milestone and does not introduce a new
feature milestone.

| `.planning/debug/resolved/teaching-design-cover-hours.md` | Preserve `H` in `计划总课时` and task `课时`, and align cover labels so four-character and five-character labels share the same colon column. | Resolved and user-accepted | pending |

## Root Cause

The post-close review found that the package-owned `teaching-design` renderer
had two output-format regressions:

- it reused a total-hour formatter that removed the trailing `H`, so the cover
  showed `160` instead of `160H`; and
- it rendered task-analysis hours as raw numeric values, so `课时` rows showed
  `40`/`60` instead of `40H`/`60H`.

The cover metadata labels were also emitted as whole text runs. That made
four-character labels narrower than the five-character `计划总课时` label, so
the colons did not align like the accepted template sample.

## Fix

`skills/teaching-design-package/scripts/teaching-design-renderer.js` now:

- formats cover and task-analysis hour values through `hourLabel()`, preserving
  or adding a trailing uppercase `H`;
- emits cover labels through a package-owned `coverLabelCell()` helper that
  lays characters out in a fixed-width grid, so four-character and
  five-character labels share `cover-label-width` and align their colons; and
- keeps the fix inside `teaching-design-package`, without modifying or calling
  legacy `skills/jiaoan-shicao`.
