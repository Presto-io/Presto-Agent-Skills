# Phase 10 Plan: Ordered Reveals and Classroom Answer Masks

## Goal

Deliver the smallest classroom-playback slice for ordered reveal groups, emphasis animation, and answer masks in the existing offline HTML renderer.

## Scope

In scope:

- Markdown-first block reveal syntax with explicit `order`.
- Markdown-first inline/block answer masks with explicit `order`.
- Markdown-first inline/block emphasis markers with explicit `order`.
- Manifest `reveal_steps` with deterministic `step_index`, original `priority`, kinds, and target counts.
- Playback navigation that advances reveal steps before advancing pages, and reverses steps before returning to the prior page.
- Verification fixture covering non-top-to-bottom order, equal-order simultaneous targets, emphasized answers, and masked answers.

Deferred:

- hover/peek pointer interactions.
- SmartArt, timeline, gallery, card-board, or other structured layout systems.
- automatic semantic icons.
- sort-question final reordering.
- generic body/paragraph animation modes.
- section-divider display toggles and export/print-specific controls.

## Tasks

1. Review current uncommitted changes against SP-14, SP-15, and SP-16.
2. Trim the canonical skill docs and template fixture to the minimal Phase 10 behavior.
3. Keep the renderer focused on `reveal`, `mask`, and `emphasis` as active Phase 10 interaction kinds.
4. Update verification so the black-box gate proves repeatability, hierarchy, workspace playback, and reveal/mask/emphasis behavior only.
5. Record automated verification evidence and close Phase 10 planning state.

## Acceptance Criteria

- `skills/school-presentation/SKILL.md` documents reveal, mask, and emphasis as the Phase 10 authoring surface.
- `skills/school-presentation/templates/school-presentation-full.md` includes a classroom reveal/mask/emphasis fixture and does not include hover/peek, SmartArt, automatic icons, sort, or generic body animation examples.
- `skills/school-presentation/scripts/school-presentation.sh verify --workdir <dir>` passes and writes a verification manifest.
- The verification manifest reports `repeatable_html`, `hierarchy_verified`, `workspace_verified`, `reveal_verified`, and `flat_slide_compat_verified` as true.
